Every time I spend some time abroad and I come back in Italy, there is one thing that I notice very clearly of Italian landscapes and environment: no matter where you are, if you look around, you will find a campanile somewhere.
The Campanile Projects aims to grab the myriad of those little landscapes and collect them in a common place where a community of people, you, can participate to add new views, rate and comment the existing ones, grow the The Campanile Community.

 

The Campanile Project wants to be an open project that grows with the help of its community and for its community. For this reason all the material of the project, web pages included are licensed under a Creatives Commons License (see below).
